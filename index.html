using Leela Chess Zero on Lichess

Leela Chess Zero is currently one of the most accurate chess engines in the world.

This webpage allows for Leela to assist you in your chess pursuits by helping you analyse your games in Lichess.

To use Leela on Lichess, you may click the button below and follow the instructions below.

After clicking the button and following the steps on screen to give Leela access to your game analyses, you can choose her on the Lichess analysis page or in a game you’ve already finished playing.

To do so, on the three‐bar menu at the bottom of the move list, scroll down to the “Engine Manager” section, and switch from “Lichess” to “Leela Chess Zero”. That’s it! Now you should be getting help from Leela to analyse your games.
